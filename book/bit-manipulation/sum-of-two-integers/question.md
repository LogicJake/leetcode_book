## Sum of Two Integers  
### 链接  
https://leetcode.com/problems/sum-of-two-integers/description/  
### 问题描述
Calculate the sum of two integers *a* and *b*, but you are **not allowed** to use the operator `+` and `-`.

**Example:**<br />
Given *a* = 1 and *b* = 2, return 3.
